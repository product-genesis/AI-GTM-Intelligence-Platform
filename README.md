# Brewra – AI GTM Intelligence Platform

## Overview

Brewra is an AI-native GTM Intelligence platform designed to help B2B SaaS teams identify high-intent prospects, surface buying signals, and generate actionable go-to-market insights using AI-powered workflows.

As Product Manager, I led product discovery, MVP definition, prototyping, and AI feature evaluation while collaborating closely with engineering and design teams.

**PROBLEM**

Modern sales and GTM teams rely on multiple disconnected tools like CRMs, prospecting platforms, and enrichment tools.

While these tools provide data, they fail to answer the most critical question:

Which leads actually matter right now, and why?

> 
> 
> 
> Teams don’t struggle with finding leads.
> They struggle with knowing which leads matter and why.
> 

**Key challenges**

- Data is fragmented across tools
- Market insights are not actionable
- Teams spend excessive time on manual research
- No clear connection between signals and outreach
